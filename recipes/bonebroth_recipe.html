Bone Broth

Description

A hearty and healthy boney boost to your health and appetite!

Ingredients

  • meaty bones
  • onions
  • carrots
  • thyme
  • salt
  • celery

Steps

  1. Fry meaty bones and diced onions in large pot
  2. Add boiling water and bring the pot to a rolling boil
  3. Once boiling, turn heat down to simmer. Allow to simmer for 2-4 hours
  4. Add chopped carrots and chopped celery, simmer for 20 minutes. Add salt to taste
  5. Et voila! C'est bonne!
