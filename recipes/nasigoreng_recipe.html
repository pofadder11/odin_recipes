Nasi Goreng

Description

Fried rice, Indonesian-style! Your tastebuds will thank you; your arteries not so much...

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice
  • Spring onions
  • 3 Eggs
  • Chillies
  • Lots of palm oil

Steps

  1. Heat a frying pan containg about 150ml of oil until hot but no too hot
  2. Add rice and chopped spring onion
  3. Add eggs and chopped chillies
  4. Fry until all egg is cooked.
  5. The dish is complete! Enjoy
